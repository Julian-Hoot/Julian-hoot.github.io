Julian Hoot

Music Producer and Metal Drummer

Artist Bio

Julian Hoot is a producer, engineer, multi-instrumentalist, and metal drummer who will stop at nothing to create the biggest and most powerful sounds! His drumming is deeply influenced by the drumming of metal titans like Matt Halpern and Matt Garstka. His writing and production are inspired by bands like ERRA, Spritbox, and Vildhjarta. And his engineering is influenced by great producers and mixers such as Buster Odeholm and Daniel Braunstein.
The emotion of the music Julian creates is his number one priority. Whether it’s tracking guitars over and over again, drawing in precise automation lines, or shaping the breath of a vocal take, the details are what matter to Julian. He firmly believes that you can’t look over the fine details and still produce a professional sounding record that evokes an emotional response.
Julian process of producing a number of records including his hardcore band Payphone Confession’s debut EP “Something Unforgivable.”
On top of writing and producing music, Julian is always looking for new bands and artists to mix for. Julian also continues to play with Payphone Confession as they roll out their EP.
